Personal Family Tree Photo Album

How it works

The album is centred around one person. The app opens to their photo. From there, they can move through their family tree using swipes or the navigation buttons — older generations above, younger generations below, siblings side by side. One button returns to the home person at any time. Once set up, the album is completely private. No internet connection is ever required.

1

Use a PIN to enter setup mode

Add photos and names across six generations — grandparents down to great-grandchildren. Full size photos, one per frame. A name can be added to each.

2

Give it to the user

Add the photos and names you want, then give it to your loved one. You can make additions and edits to the album anytime.

3

Navigate their family tree

Swipe or use the on-screen buttons. Older generations above, younger generations below, siblings side by side.

4

Completely private

No Wi-Fi, no accounts, no cloud. All photos stay on the device.

Features

Six generations

Grandparents, parents, the home person and siblings, children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren.

Full size photos

One photo per frame. A name can be added to each.

Simple navigation

Swipe or use the on-screen buttons. Older generations above, younger below, siblings side by side.

PIN-protected setup

Only someone with the PIN can add, edit, or remove photos and names. The user just sees photos.

Auto-launch on startup *

The app can be set to open automatically when the device is turned on. Most devices supported — see FAQs.

Completely private

No internet connection required. No accounts. Photos stay on the device.
